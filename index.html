TCNM — Trusted Communication & Notification Management

TCNM is a communication startup project developed by Meder Sairakunov. The project focuses on improving digital communication infrastructure through secure messaging, smart alerts, and automated notification systems — especially in public sector services.

TCNM is currently being integrated with WhatsApp Business API to provide real-time notification services and interaction tools for users, including government employees and service recipients.
